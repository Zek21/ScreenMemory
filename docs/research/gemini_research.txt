5. Risks & Challenges
Despite the efficiency, tokenized stocks introduce new risk vectors:

Counterparty Risk: Investors are reliant on Backed Assets and the custodian holding the physical shares. If the issuer fails, the tokens may become "orphaned" from their value.

Atomic Settlement vs. Netting: SEC Commissioner Crenshaw has noted that "netting eliminates 98% of trade obligations." By settling every trade individually (atomic), capital efficiency actually decreases for large institutions who must pre-fund every trade.

Regulatory Crackdown: If xStocks begin leaking into the U.S. via DeFi "bridges" or DEXs (like Raydium or Jupiter), a significant regulatory response from the SEC/FINRA is likely.
